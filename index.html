Milestones through the evoulotion of Tech.

The Electronic Numerical Integrator and Computer, or ENIAC, was built by John Presper Eckert and John W. Mauchly at the University of Pennsylvania in 46.

IBM launched its personal computer in 1981. While IBM’s was not the first PC its version had became popular and it set the standards of what a PC should be. Costing over 1K, the IBM had only had a 40K of read-only memory and 16K of user memory. Some other features included a built in speaker and the ability to check itself.

Steve Jobs launched the first Macintosh in 84. The Apple co-founder showed a number of the Macintosh’s abilities, from its ability to “talk” as well as it ability drawing sophisticated images.

In 89 Tim Berners-Lee submitted a proposal for a way to share information at CERN. A year later, the world’s first website and server came to life at CERN.

Launched within weeks of each other in 94, the Sega Saturn and Sony PlayStation consoles became blaze trails in the video game industry. While the PlayStation has outlasted the Saturn it as seen through its multiple iterations, including the PlayStation 5 wich is set to hit markets in the fall of 2020.
